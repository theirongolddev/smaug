How to make $1M in 2026 using Claude Memory (step by step business plan)

Claude just launched memory imports. Here is the step-by-step playbook to build a $1M/yr business.

Claude just launched memory imports.

That means you can migrate anyone from ChatGPT to Claude in 60 seconds.

The idea is simple. 
Claude Migration as a Service. 
sounds basic... it is. anyone can do this idea...

Most businesses have employees on personal ChatGPT accounts. No shared context, connected workflows, company knowledge baked in. They are playing LLMs on hard mode.

This is where the Claude ecosystem comes into play. It's better for businesses IMO with Projects, memory, skills, cowork, connectors, MCP integrations. 

it's quickly becomes a business operating system, not a chatbot.

The playbook:

1. Pick a vertical. Pick a specific team in that vertical. 

I'm talking like sales teams at automotive dealerships. 
Or buy-side agents at commercial real estate brokerages. 

Get weird specific. Being general is death these days.

2. Record their workflows with Wispr Flow. Transcribe their meetings. Document every process and SOP they run on muscle memory.

3. Turn those SOPs into Claude skills.

4. Turn those skills into agents.

5. Build them a dashboard so they react to their business instead of manually running it.

You're not selling "AI consulting." You're selling autonomy.

How you get customers:

→ Create content and lead magnets specific to that vertical ("How AI is replacing the BDR role at car dealerships") 
→ Get organic working then pour on some ad spend to drive the audience to your lead magnet
→ Run a free workshop introducing them to Claude's ecosystem and why it beats ChatGPT for business 
→ Upsell a paid lunch-and-learn training on-site

Now you are in the door and making money with a wedge offer.

The money:

Workshops: $2,500–$10,000 per engagement 
Documentation + process mapping retainer: $5,000/month 
Custom agents + software dashboards: $10,000/month
Maintenance fees for managing the systems $1,000/mo

The math to $1M:

Months 1–3: Land 5 workshop clients → $25K. Convert 2 to retainers → $10K/mo.

Total by month 10: $1M+ (conservative)

You don't need 1,000 clients. You need 15 good ones in one niche.
