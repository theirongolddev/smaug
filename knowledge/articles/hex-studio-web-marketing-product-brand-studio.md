## Summary

HEX Studio is an experimental creative studio that partners with cutting-edge technology companies to create monopolizing brands and complete digital experiences. Their approach combines creative direction, web design, brand strategy, and product design into full-cycle projects, emphasizing craftsmanship, velocity, and collaboration through a lean micro-squad model. 150+ projects completed, $1B+ raised by clients post-engagement.

## Key Takeaways

- **Full-cycle creative services:** Brand identity, Webflow development, visual systems, and marketing materials in 6-12 weeks
- **Specialized squad approach:** Leverages animators, illustrators, copywriters, and developers for detailed execution
- **Proven track record:** 150+ completed projects with $1B+ raised by clients post-engagement
- **Flexible engagement models:** Fixed-bid projects, monthly subscriptions ($8k/month), or long-term partnerships
- **Design philosophy:** 14 Laws emphasizing iteration, velocity without haste, focused execution, and overdelivering for clients
