Quiver AI is a new AI lab and product company focused on frontier vector design. Founded by Joan Rodriguez, the company raised an $8.3M seed round led by Andreessen Horowitz (a16z). Their flagship product, Arrow-1.0, is an AI model that generates scalable vector graphics (SVGs) from images and text, now available in public beta.

## Key Takeaways

- **Arrow-1.0**: First model generates SVGs from images and text with AI
- **Funding**: $8.3M seed round led by a16z with additional angel and institutional support
- **Focus**: Frontier vector design represents a new frontier in AI-powered design tools
- **Status**: Available now in public beta at app.quiver.ai
- **Use Case**: Enables designers and developers to programmatically create vector graphics using AI
