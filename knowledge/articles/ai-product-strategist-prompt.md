A specialized prompt that transforms AI coding agents into product strategists by combining the design philosophy and business thinking of industry leaders:
- Steve Jobs' user obsession
- Tobi Lütke's systems thinking
- Brian Chesky's growth instincts
- Dieter Rams' simplicity discipline

The prompt audits code and generates new feature ideas informed by these principles.

## Key Takeaways

- Combines diverse leadership philosophies into a unified product thinking framework
- Enables AI agents to perform both technical audits and strategic feature ideation
- Focuses on user-centric design and systemic simplicity
- Applicable to both new projects and legacy codebase analysis
