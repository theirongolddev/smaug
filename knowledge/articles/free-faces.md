## Summary

Free Faces is a curated gallery of open-source and freely-licensed typefaces available for download and use. The collection is designed for UI/UX designers and developers seeking high-quality fonts that can be used legally in both personal and commercial projects without licensing complications.

## Key Takeaways

- **Curated collection:** Hand-selected typefaces under various permissive free licenses
- **Commercial-friendly:** All fonts can be used in personal and commercial projects
- **No licensing headaches:** Clear licensing terms for each typeface
- **Designer-focused resource:** Built specifically for UI/UX professionals seeking reliable font options
