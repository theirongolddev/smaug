A practical template that teaches Claude to write in your authentic voice, eliminating generic AI language patterns. The approach combines pre-built writing rules and banned phrases with your own writing samples for pattern matching. Takes approximately 10 minutes to set up.

## Key Takeaways

**Pre-Configured Rules:** 95% of the template is ready-to-use, including writing rules, banned phrases, and formatting standards. Eliminates the most obvious AI-isms immediately.

**Writing Rules Focus:** Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences), contractions, specific details, varied sentence length, natural transitions, honest hedging, physical verbs, and parenthetical asides. Emphasis on sounding like a sharp human, not a language model.

**Comprehensive Banned Phrases List:** Covers dead AI language (harness, leverage, delve), mechanical transitions (furthermore, additionally), engagement bait (let that sink in), AI cringe (supercharge, unlock), generic insider claims, and the "fatal" pattern of negation-then-assertion ("This isn't X. This is Y.").

**Writing Samples Are Critical:** Paste examples of your own writing—Google Docs, reports, proposals, sent emails, Slack messages, old blog posts. More samples = better voice match. Crucially, use pre-AI writing to capture your authentic voice before it blended with Claude's defaults.

**How It Works:** Claude pattern-matches against your samples, learning your actual voice rather than generic templates. Setup is one-time; every subsequent session reads the template before you say a word.

## Key Features

- One-file setup (copy, add writing samples, save)
- Pre-configured writing rules and formatting standards
- Comprehensive list of overused AI phrases to ban
- Pattern-matching against your own writing samples
- Works as a Claude Workspace context file
- Takes ~10 minutes to set up
- Results in indistinguishable AI-generated and human-written drafts
