Intercom made a public commitment 16 months ago to double R&D productivity in 9 months. They hit it in 6 months. More remarkable: they've actually achieved 3× productivity gains overall.

This case study reveals the full implementation story behind widespread Claude Code adoption across the organization. 1,100 of Intercom's 1,305 employees are now active Claude Code users—not just R&D teams, but finance, sales, legal, recruiting, and other departments building reports, tools, and prototypes that would have been impossible or required days of work before.

The post covers the data, the nuance of what they measured beyond pull requests, and acknowledges what's still messy or uncertain. Part 2 (forthcoming) will detail the actual implementation process, lessons learned, and their roadmap forward.

## Key Takeaways

- Achieved 3× productivity gains in 16 months (targeted 2×, hit it in 9 months)
- 1,100 of 1,305 employees are active Claude Code users across all departments
- Non-R&D teams (finance, sales, legal, recruiting) building tools/reports/prototypes themselves, reducing dependencies and cycle time
- Measured rigorously beyond traditional metrics (PRs); includes nuance and complexity
- Organizational change at scale: 84% adoption rate indicates successful tooling and cultural shift
- Honest assessment of what's still uncertain or messy in the implementation
