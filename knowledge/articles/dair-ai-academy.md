DAIR.AI Academy is an online learning platform from the team behind DAIR.AI (democratizing AI research), offering practical courses on AI agents, Claude Code, RAG, LLMs, and fine-tuning. Targets builders who want hands-on, production-focused AI skills rather than academic theory. Free tier available; Pro tier at $29/mo (billed annually) includes all courses, certificates, live events, and an AI tutor.

## Key Takeaways
- Courses focused on AI agents, RAG, LLM development, and Claude Code — practical builder orientation
- Freemium model: free courses available, Pro unlocks full library + certificates + live workshops
- Community-driven with peer feedback and AI tutor support
- Team pricing for 5+ seats with dedicated onboarding
- Student pricing available on request
