## Summary

Every is a subscription service offering curated ideas, apps, and training for founders, operators, and investors who want to stay at the edge of AI. The service bundles educational content with tool recommendations and training focused on AI adoption and strategy. Offers a 30-day free trial to reduce adoption friction and builds on a community of 100,000+ subscribers including leaders at major companies.

## Key Takeaways

- **Target audience**: Founders, operators, and investors focused on AI-powered business growth
- **Bundled value**: Single subscription providing ideas, apps, and training eliminates subscription fatigue
- **Free trial strategy**: 30-day trial removes adoption friction and enables sampling before commitment
- **Community as product**: 100,000+ subscriber base provides networking and social proof
- **Trusted positioning**: Endorsements from recognized company leaders (shared logos)
- **Brand promise**: "The only subscription you need to stay at the edge of AI" positions as comprehensive solution
- **Content focus**: Emphasis on staying current with rapidly evolving AI landscape
