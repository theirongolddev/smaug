Jeffrey Emanuel describes replacing $2,000+/year CPA-prepared tax returns with an AI-assisted workflow. Originally written about Claude Opus 3.7, the technique applies to current models. The core claim: AI can do error-prone manual tax prep work when given structured financial documents, and do it more cheaply than a human professional.

## Key Takeaways
- AI can replace $2,000+/year CPA fees for individual tax preparation
- Structured document ingestion (1099s, W-2s, investment statements) is the key workflow step
- Error rates may be lower than human preparers for well-structured inputs
- Written for Opus 3.7 but applicable to any capable Claude model
- Most relevant during tax season (Jan–April)
